LUCKY 33 game Rules:
Only 10 participants per game. Each player bets 33 DOGE, hence total game pool is 330 DOGE.
During the draw the system will randomly distribute the funds on stake depending on a type of the game that was selected.
Normal Profit game:
- 8 Lucky Players win 40 DOGE each.
- 2 Other Players lose.
- System fee per game is 10 DOGE deducted from total game pool
2X Profit game:
- 5 Lucky Players win 63 DOGE each.
- 5 Other Players lose.
- System fee per game is 15 DOGE deducted from total game pool
10X Profit game:
- 1 Lucky Player wins 310 DOGE.
- 9 Other Players lose.
- System fee per game is 20 DOGE deducted from total game pool
The draw is held once all 10 players enter the game. Game results are shown right after the game's end. Prizes are credited to winners' account balance instantly right after the game's end.

- One player can take part in one game at a time.
- A player can take part in all three game types simultaneously, but not multiple times in the same game type at once.
- Once the bet is made, the bid cannot be canceled.
- If the player has enough funds to participate in the game, entrance fee (33 DOGE) is withdrawn from his balance once as he presses START THE GAME button.
- If the player does not have enough money to participate in the game, he can claim FREE DOGE or refill his account balance.
